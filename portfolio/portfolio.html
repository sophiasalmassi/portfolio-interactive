A Few Things About Design

Design is an art form, a method of human expression that follows a system of highly developed procedures in order
to create objects, high tech performances, and experiences with newer methods.
Like all art forms, design has the potential to solve problems, give solutions to society and humans

Why Design

Designe takes a big place in our day to day lifes and without even necessarily realizing it, we are constantly in touch and surrounded by objects, interfaces and performances thoroughly thought to solve social and environmental issues related to our present world. It takes a lot of creativity and open-mindedness but also, empathy and a great connection to people.

Why Strate
